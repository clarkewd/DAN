rooms & suites

Each of the 90 spacious and elegantly decorated rooms and suites have been completely renovated in 2012. All have been exquisitely appointed to create the perfect environment for any visit – be it business, pleasure or entertainment.Features include walk-in closets, grand bathrooms and individual state-of-the-art in-room climate control, entertainment and technology. Many of the deluxe rooms, junior suites and suites offer balconies. Smoking rooms available upon request.

The Royal Suite, featuring a living space of more than 250 square meters (2,690 square feet), has lavish fabrics and furnishings. Overlooking one of the city’s most classic squares, Kongens Nytorv, the Royal Suite offers a breathtaking view of the Royal Danish Theatre and Nyhavn.
